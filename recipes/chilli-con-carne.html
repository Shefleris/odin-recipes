Chilli con carne

This is a rather simple and easy chilli con carne recipe.

It's a perfect dish to serve at casual get-togethers or other gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • Minced beef 500g
  • Can of chopped tomatoes 1
  • Tomato puree
  • Can of red kidney beans
  • Red bell pepper 1
  • Large onion 1
  • Beef stock cube 1
  • Spices to taste:
    • Paprika
    • Chilli flakes
    • Ground cumin
    • Coriander leaves

optional:

  • Rice
  • Pasta

Steps:

  1. Heat a bit of oil in a large pot. Fry the onions and garlic until they are soft.
  2. Add the minced beef into the pot and fry it untill it's brow. Make sure to break up any chunks of meat with a spoon.
  3. Add the can of tomatoes, tomato puree, the spices as well as the stock cube
  4. Bring the whole pot to simmer, cover with a lid and let it cook over a gentle heat.
  5. Add the kidney beans and bell pepper. Cook for additional 10min
  6. Serve with rice, pasta or just by itself
