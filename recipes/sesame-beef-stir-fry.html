Sesame beef stir fry

Sesame beef stir fry, with it's robust tender meat and symphony of flavours from the sacred sessame, served atop a bed of steaming rice, is worthy to be served to any of our warriors.

It is another very simple dish that never ceases to amaze me. For it's very flexible and can easily be adapted to whatever ingredients you have.

Ingredients

  • Beef strips 500g
  • Bell pepper
  • Snow peas
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Sesame seeds
  • Spices, sauce:
    • Black pepper
    • Salt
    • Soy sauce
    • Oyster sauce
    • Sweet chilli sauce

Optional

  • Rice (basmati, jasmin)
  • Rice noodles
  • Egg noodles
  • Pasta

Steps:

  1. Combine the beef, spices, sauce, sesame seeds in a bowl. Leave it to marinade
  2. Remove the beef from marinade
  3. Fry the garlic and onions until soft
  4. Add beef until it's cooked. Put it to the side.
  5. Fry the rest of the vegetables on a high heat, for a short bit
  6. Add back the beef and the left over marinade, let it simmer for a bit
  7. It's done. Enjoy the dish.
